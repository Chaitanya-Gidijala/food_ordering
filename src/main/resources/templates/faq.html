FAQ - FoodHub

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We're here to help you.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email confirmation containing your live tracking link. You can also log in to your account and navigate to the My Orders section to view real-time courier updates.

What is your return/refund policy?

We handle refunds on a case-by-case basis. If your food arrives damaged, incorrect, or drastically late, please contact support within 1 hour of delivery for a full refund or credit.

Do you offer contactless delivery?

Yes, absolutely. You can select "Contactless Delivery" at checkout. Our driver will leave your order at your doorstep and notify you when it has been delivered.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

We process orders immediately to ensure freshness. You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After that, the kitchen has likely started preparing your food.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, and various digital wallets. All transactions are securely encrypted.
